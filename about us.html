What is TWAMBI MOTORS?


Nice to meet you! We are Twambimotors Africa.
We are a group company of a listed company in Japan. Our parent company is called Twambimotors
Holdings,and we provide the best internet financial services in Japan.The Twambimotors Group has
numerous group companies in Japan and overseas,mainly in the financial services business including
Twambimotors Securities and Twambimotors Sumishin Net Bank,the leading online financial services
companies,the asset management business, and the biotechnology,healthcare and medical informatics
business.Twambimotors Africa is one of them.


Our Mission


Many Japanese used cars are imported to Africa, but not all of them are of high quality.
Some of the cars have large dents or engines that fail quickly. However, we want to enrich
the lives of people in Africa and contribute to the economic development of the region.
